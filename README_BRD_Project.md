### 📌 What This Project Demonstrates

This project showcases how a **Business Analyst can use Generative AI as a co-pilot** to accelerate the creation of a professional Business Requirements Document — while maintaining the domain expertise, stakeholder empathy, and critical judgment that only a human BA brings.

**This is NOT a document written by AI.** It is a document written by a Business Analyst *with* AI — and every section transparently shows:

1. 🟣 **The AI prompt I engineered** (prompt engineering skill)
2. 🔵 **The raw AI output** (what the AI generated)
3. 🟢 **My human-edited final version** (where I applied domain expertise, corrected assumptions, and added specificity)

---

### 🏥 The Project: SmartSchedule AI

**SmartSchedule AI** is a fictional AI-powered patient scheduling platform designed for mid-size US hospital systems (200–500 beds).

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Problem** | US hospitals lose $150K–$300K/year per facility to scheduling inefficiencies — no-shows average 18–25%, provider utilization sits at ~68%, and patients wait 23+ minutes |
| **Solution** | ML-powered no-show prediction, dynamic schedule optimization, intelligent patient self-scheduling, automated waitlist backfill |
| **Target Customer** | Hospital operations directors at mid-size systems |
| **Key Integrations** | Epic, Cerner/Oracle Health via HL7 FHIR R4 APIs |
| **Compliance** | HIPAA, HITECH, SOC 2 Type II |

---

### 📄 BRD Contents

The complete BRD includes:

- **Executive Summary** — with AI prompt → raw output → human-edited comparison
- **SMART Business Objectives** — 5 measurable objectives tied to hospital KPIs
- **Project Scope** — In scope, out of scope, and assumptions
- **Stakeholder Analysis Matrix** — 7 stakeholders with interest, influence, concerns, and communication plans
- **Functional Requirements** — 8 detailed requirements with IDs, priorities, acceptance criteria, and dependencies
- **Non-Functional Requirements** — Security, performance, availability, AI explainability, and compliance
- **User Stories** — 6 stories covering operations director, coordinator, physician, patient, and compliance officer perspectives
- **Risk Assessment** — 5 risks with probability, impact, mitigation strategies, and owners
- **Success Metrics** — Baseline vs. target with measurement methods
- **Implementation Timeline** — 5-phase, 18-week rollout plan
- **AI Methodology Reflections** — Lessons learned on where AI excelled vs. where human judgment was critical
